React Development

Building a shopping cart interface in React

Introduction

In this project, I developed a cake shopping interface in React, and learned to use various React tools such as class components, stateless components, and hooks.

The final product is the website of an online cake shop display, where users can browse through a list of cakes, filter items by categories, sort the items on display, and add or remove any number of item to a shopping cart.

Design

Many design and usability principles were considered throughout the creation of this interface:

  • The filters, as well as the total price of items in the cart, are fixed at the top of the page for visibility and accessibility.
  • Labels are in a smaller font underneath the title in order to convey information hierarchy, and are highlighted according to their categories for visual indications of different categorizations.
  • Item cards in the cart are smaller and contain less information to reduce unnecessary clutter, but are styled similarly to item cards the “Browse” section provide consistency
